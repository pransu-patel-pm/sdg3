About This Platform

This web-based system is designed to record and monitor common health issues and environmental problems in a village. The goal is to support preventive health care by identifying patterns related to illness, sanitation, and surroundings.

All data collected is anonymous and used only for awareness, monitoring, and planning purposes.

Why Preventive Health Matters

Many health problems do not appear suddenly. They develop over time due to factors such as poor sanitation, stagnant water, improper waste disposal, and seasonal changes.

By observing early signs and environmental conditions, communities can take action before illnesses become serious.

SDG-3: Good Health & Well-Being

This system supports Sustainable Development Goal 3, which aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all.

  • Encourages early identification of health risks
  • Promotes clean surroundings and hygiene
  • Supports community-level health awareness
  • Focuses on prevention rather than treatment

How This System Helps

  • Records common health issues without personal identification
  • Allows reporting of garbage and cleanliness problems
  • Creates awareness about links between environment and health
  • Supports data-based preventive planning

Disclaimer: This system does not diagnose diseases or prescribe medicines. It is intended only for data recording and health awareness.
